UX/UI Designer

I apply user-centered design principles to create intuitive and well-structured digital experiences. My skills include understanding user needs, organizing information, designing user flows, wireframes, and interfaces with a focus on usability, visual hierarchy, and consistency.

Tools

I work mainly with Figma throughout my design process, from early wireframes to high-fidelity interfaces and prototypes. I’m currently learning to use Adobe tools and remain open to exploring new tools and technologies that can support and improve my design workflow.

Communication

Through my experience working with customer service and as an English teacher, I developed strong communication skills and the ability to explain ideas clearly, listen actively, and adapt my approach to different people and contexts. These experiences strongly influence the way I collaborate and design with empathy.

Main project

TravelGo

TravelGo is a mobile travel planning application designed to simplify and centralize the trip planning experience.

The project focused on reducing fragmentation by bringing all travel details into one place, using AI to generate personalized itineraries while still allowing manual customization.

A key focus was enabling collaboration for group trips and creating a seamless flow by integrating trusted booking platforms.
